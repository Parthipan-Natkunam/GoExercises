## Question
Given the non-negative integer `n` , output the value of its [hyperfactorial](https://mathworld.wolfram.com/Hyperfactorial.html). Don't worry about outputs exceeding your language's integer limit.

### Example
```typescript
> hyperfactorial(0)
> 1

> hyperfactorial(2)
> 4

> hyperfactorial(3)
> 108

> hyperfactorial(7)
> 3319766398771200000
```
